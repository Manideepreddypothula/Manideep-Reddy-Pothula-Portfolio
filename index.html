Welcome to my digital space!

I'm Manideep Reddy Pothula, a passionate Healthcare Data Analyst with a strong drive to improve patient outcomes and healthcare operations through data-driven insights, machine learning, and AI. I recently graduated with a Master's degree in Biomedical and Health Informatics from the State University of New York at Oswego, achieving a 3.98 GPA.

My academic and project work focused on building clinical decision support systems and predictive models for chronic conditions such as heart disease, diabetes, and breast cancer. I'm particularly interested in using data science to enhance decision-making, reduce inefficiencies, and support personalized medicine in real-world clinical settings.

I bring a solid foundation in Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn), SQL, Power BI, and Tableau. I also have experience working with electronic health records (EHR), public health datasets, and healthcare reporting tools. My strengths lie in identifying trends, uncovering actionable insights, and translating data into impactful solutions.
